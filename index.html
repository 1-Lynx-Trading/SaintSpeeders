About The Team

Built for Speed.
Engineered for Victory.

ST Speeders Racing Team represents the next generation of motorsport engineering. Founded on the principles of innovation, sustainability, and sheer performance, we design, build, and race hydrogen-powered vehicles that push the boundaries of what's possible.

Hydrogen Fuel Cell

Our hydrogen fuel cell system produces zero emissions while delivering exceptional power output. Clean energy, uncompromised performance.

Aerodynamic Excellence

Every surface is optimised through computational fluid dynamics, ensuring maximum downforce with minimal drag for cornering dominance.

Advanced Telemetry

Real-time data acquisition and analysis systems monitor every parameter, giving our drivers the information advantage on track.

Precision Engineering

Manufactured to tolerances measured in microns. Our carbon fibre chassis and 3D-printed components are race-proven technology.

Sustainable Racing

Leading the transition to sustainable motorsport. Hydrogen power means zero carbon emissions — racing that doesn't cost the earth.

Race-Ready Mentality

Every team member is driven by the same goal — standing on the top step of the podium at the H2GP Victorian Race 2026.

Carbon Fibre Construction

Our monocoque chassis utilises aerospace-grade carbon fibre composite, achieving an exceptional strength-to-weight ratio.

H2GP Series

Hydrogen Grand
Prix 2026

The H2GP (Hydrogen Grand Prix) is the world's premier hydrogen fuel cell racing series. Teams design, build, and race hydrogen-powered remote-controlled vehicles, competing in endurance races that test engineering excellence, strategic thinking, and teamwork.

PHASE 01 — COMPLETE

Team Formation & Research

Assembled the team, conducted initial hydrogen fuel cell research, and established our engineering methodology.

PHASE 02 — IN PROGRESS

Design & Manufacturing

CAD modelling, CFD simulation, carbon fibre layup, 3D printing of components, and fuel cell integration.

PHASE 03 — UPCOMING

Testing & Optimisation

Track testing, telemetry analysis, aerodynamic tuning, and race strategy development.

PHASE 04 — TARGET

Victorian Race 2026

Race day. Everything we've built for. The checkered flag awaits.
